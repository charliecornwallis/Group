    Our group is interested in explaining the origin and breakdown of complex life<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Life on earth has been shaped by several major evolutionary transitions each of which has involved independent replicating units joining together to form a new, more complex life form. For example, transitions to multicellularity, sexual reproduction, group living (societies) and symbiotic existence have all played fundamental roles in the development of life.<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Our research focuses on investigating <\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    (1) Why do transitions occur?<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    (2) How complex life is maintained?<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    (3) What role do transformations in organismal design play in transitions?<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    (4) Why do complex systems break down?<\/span><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    This involves a number of more specific projects, click here for more details.<\/a><\/p>\n\n\n\n
